Why Maldives?

The Maldives' stunning white sand beaches, glowing turquoise waters, and luxurious overwater resorts that offer unmatched relaxation are the reasons why I've always wanted to travel there. One of the main attractions on Vaadhoo Island is the exceptional chance to see the captivating "Sea of Stars" phenomenon, where bioluminescent plankton illuminates the shore. I also really want to swim with gentle whale sharks and magnificent manta rays in their natural environment because it would be an amazing wildlife experience. These outstanding natural wonders and experiences make the Maldives yet another top pick for my next trip.

Places I want to visit in Maldives:

Malé – For its vibrant city life and colorful markets.

Vaadhoo Island – To witness the stunning bioluminescent beach at night.

Ari Atoll – For world-class diving and marine life encounters.

Getting There:

Traveling from Quezon City, Philippines to the Maldives requires taking a connecting flight from Ninoy Aquino International Airport (MNL) in Manila to Velana International Airport (MLE) in Malé. Common layover cities include Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, and Colombo, with various airlines offering these routes. Philippine citizens typically receive a 30-day tourist visa on arrival in the Maldives, provided they have the necessary travel documents. It's crucial to book flights and accommodation in advance, and if staying on a resort island, arrange airport transfers with the property. Therefore, the only practical way to reach the Maldives from Quezon City is by air, involving a connecting flight and proper preparation of travel essentials.
