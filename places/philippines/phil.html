Why Philippines?

Exploring the Philippines will always be one of my dream destinations, as it is the country where I was born and grew up. I have always wanted to experience the various traditions and cultures found throughout the Philippines. It would be a true honor and privilege to witness and appreciate the beauty of my homeland. Each region offers its own unique charm, from stunning beaches to vibrant festivals. I look forward to discovering the rich history and warm hospitality that make the Philippines so special.

Places I want to visit in Philippines:

Bohol – Known for its iconic Chocolate Hills, pristine beaches, and the adorable tarsiers.

Cebu – Famous for its beautiful beaches, diving spots, and the historic Magellan's Cross.

Sagada – Renowned for its scenic mountain views, hanging coffins, and rich cultural heritage.

Getting There:

To be able to go to Bohol and Cebu, tourists usually go there by air or sea. Nevertheless, tourists can go to these places by land, but it will take them a lot of time, and they would still be required to board a ship. For Sagada, tourists can go there by land just by riding a bus from Cubao or using their personal vehicles. The scenic views along the route make the journey worthwhile, offering glimpses of the beautiful countryside. Additionally, once in these destinations, visitors can explore stunning landscapes and immerse themselves in local culture and traditions.
