My kind of Movies

A collection of my favorite movies & TV Shows

Crime

The GodFather

Director: Francis Ford Coppola

Main Character: Al Pacino (Michael Corleone)

Release Year: 1972

Synposis: "The Godfather" chronicles the Corleone family, a powerful New York Mafia dynasty, as they navigate a treacherous world of organized crime, power struggles, and family loyalty. The story follows Michael Corleone's transformation from a reluctant outsider to the ruthless head of the family.

Action

John Wick

Director: Chad Stahelski (with David Leitch credited as co-director for the first film)

Main Character: Keanu Reeves (John Wick)

Release Year: 2014

Synposis: After the death of his wife, John Wick's grief is compounded when his vintage car is stolen and his dog, a final gift from his wife, is killed. This sets him on a relentless path of revenge against the Russian mobsters responsible, revealing his legendary past as a feared assassin.

My Kind of TV Shows

Crime

Peaky Blinders

Director: Steven Knight

Main Character: Cillian Murphy (Thomas Shelby)

Release Year: 2013

Synposis: The series follows the Shelby family, a notorious gang known as the Peaky Blinders, led by the cunning and ambitious Thomas Shelby. They navigate the criminal underworld, seeking to expand their power and influence while facing rival gangs, police, and political adversaries.

Legal Drama

Suits

Director: Aaron Korsh

Main Character: Gabriel Macht (Harvey Specter), Patrick J. Adams (Mike Ross)

Release Year: 2011

Synposis: The series centers on Harvey Specter, a top corporate lawyer, who hires Mike Ross, a brilliant but unlicensed college dropout, as his associate. They navigate complex legal cases while keeping Mike's lack of a law degree a secret, facing challenges both professionally and personally.
